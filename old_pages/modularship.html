Modular Ship

 This a game I am developing in Processing 3 with Fisica. It is a space shooter where the user has to destroy enemy ships and can steal what parts they drop to improve his/her own ship. Right now you can add modules you your ship, fly around, and test firing on indestructible blobs or an enemy dummy ship. (this feature will be removed after enemies are added or moved to debug mode)

How to play the game:

  1. Download the game for: Windows or Mac
  2. Unzip or extract the downloaded file
  3. Double click the play icon inside OR right click and press open/run
  4. Enjoy!

Controls:

  • The movement controls are WASD with Q and E controlling strafing (sideways motion)
    • W = fowards
    • A = turn left
    • S = backwards
    • D = turn right
    • Q = move left
    • E = move right
  • Mouse click to drag ships and modules (ship parts) and attach modules to your ship
  • R rotates the orientation in which a module will be attached (shown by the outline of whatever module you have grabbed)
  • N spawns a new structural module
  • M spawns a thruster module
  • COMMA spawns a weapon module
  • B loads a preset "basic" ship if you press it before adding modules to your ship (the same ship that the dummy enemy has)
Keep in mind that your ship may not fly straight if you do not balance out the thrusters!
